About

I am a Project Manager and SSA Solutions & Customer Success Engineer with a focus on Space Situational Awareness (SSA), Space Traffic Management (STM), and operational mission support. Over the last years, I have worked across customer-facing and technical roles, combining project management, systems engineering, and business development to support commercial and institutional space missions.

At OKAPI:Orbits, I manage LEOP support services and help deliver tailored STM solutions, aligning product development with market needs and customer workflows. Previously, I worked as a Space Systems Engineer at ConstellR on verification and validation plans, subsystem coordination, and requirement flowdown, and completed a Masters thesis at The Exploration Company on an MBSE automation framework that links requirements databases with engineering data.

My earlier experience spans mission analysis for a 3U CubeSat, SAP customer success and backend support, and systems engineering & business development at Valispace, including workshops, use-case development, and an IAC 2021 publication on data-driven engineering. I am deeply passionate about using space technology to benefit life on Earth and building data-driven, scalable processes for future missions.
